# **Fake Review Detection (BERT + Ensemble) – Colab Training Pipeline**
**STEPS:**

## **1.Mounted Google Drive to save and access files easily.**

## **2.Installed required dependencies:**
pandas, numpy, scikit-learn, transformers, torch, joblib, tqdm, and nltk.

## **3.Imported necessary libraries such as BertTokenizer, BertModel, torch, and preprocessing utilities.**

## **4.Created helper scripts:**
-preprocess.py → handled text cleaning and tokenization.
-utils.py → handled data loading and splitting.
-model.py → defined training functions for ML models.

## **5.Loaded dataset and kept only valid labeled reviews.**

## **6.Generated BERT embeddings using bert-base-uncased.**

## **7.Split dataset into training (16,000 samples) and testing (4,000 samples).**

## **8.Trained three classifiers:**
-Logistic Regression (max_iter=700)
-Linear SVM (max_iter=3000)
-Random Forest (n_estimators=30)

## **9.Combined models using Voting Ensemble for final prediction.**

## **10.Evaluated model – achieved:
-Accuracy: 0.8960
-Precision: 0.8973
-Recall: 0.9983
-F1 Score: 0.9451

## **11.Saved the final trained model as bert_fake_review_model.pkl.**
